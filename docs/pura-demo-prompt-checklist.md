# Pura demo prompt checklist

Use these prompts after running the manager admin demo seed for `demo-full@purelyautomation.dev`.

## Inbox and follow-up

- Summarize my inbox and tell me what needs attention first.
- Which customer conversations still need a reply?
- Draft a reply to Sarah Miller about her quote and Thursday afternoon availability.
- What happened in the text thread with Jamie Carter?
- Which invoice or billing-related thread is in my inbox right now?

## Contacts and people

- Show me the important details you have on Sarah Miller.
- Who came in through the AI receptionist recently?
- Find the contact who asked for Thursday afternoon availability.
- What do we know about Alex Parker and the partnership conversation?
- List the contacts that look closest to booking right now.

## Tasks and priorities

- What open tasks do I have right now?
- Which tasks are already done?
- What should I handle first today based on my current tasks and inbox?
- Tell me which task is about Mike Rivera.
- Give me a quick work summary across tasks and inbox.

## Reviews and reputation

- Summarize my latest reviews.
- Do I have any reviews without a business reply?
- Which customers left the strongest feedback?
- Show me the review from Sarah Miller.
- What is my average rating in the seeded demo data?

## AI receptionist

- Summarize my recent AI receptionist calls.
- Did any recent callers ask about pricing or availability?
- Which callers left enough information for follow-up?
- What happened on the most recent AI receptionist call?
- Which AI receptionist lead seems hottest right now?

## Cross-surface prompts

- Based on my inbox, tasks, contacts, reviews, and receptionist calls, what should I do next?
- Give me a quick owner update on the demo account.
- What are the biggest opportunities sitting in this seeded account?
- Where do you see unfinished follow-up across the demo data?
- If I only had 10 minutes, what should I handle first?

## Seeded records this checklist expects

- Contacts: Sarah Miller, Mike Rivera, Jordan Lee, Jamie Carter, Alex Parker.
- Inbox threads: quote follow-up, invoice, partnership email, arrival text, reschedule text.
- Tasks: Mike callback, Alex partnership reply, invoice processing, Jamie reschedule confirmation.
- Reviews: Sarah Miller, Jamie Carter, Mike Rivera.
- AI receptionist calls: Sarah M., Mike R., Unknown caller.
